    About Us

    Marianetti Motors | About Us

    First off let me introduce myself, and thank you for reading about the company. My name is Paul Marianetti and since I was about 25 it has always been my goal to open my own luxury/exotic dealership by the age of 40. I started in the business working in the Toyota and Lexus division from 1997 through to 2010 ranging from sales, to sales management to general management. At that point I felt a new challenge was in order and moved to Porsche managing sales from 2010 through to 2015. After five years working at Porsche, upper management shifted me within the company and tapped me on the shoulder to help build McLaren Automotive’s presence in Toronto. A brand that at the time was still only in its infancy in Canada.

    Eventually, my desire to operate my own dealership came to the forefront of my mind, and in my vision was a unique, boutique-style dealership with a comfortable, VIP feel. My true passion in life is entertaining and with that also came helping people find unique, niche cars to enjoy. My goal when helping to match a client to a car is to find a car that works best for the persons life style, who’s history is as clean as when it was new, and that is also a wise financial purchase for those that like to try new things more often. After several different designs during the build, I decided to create studio-like environment that I feel also provides a welcoming, cool vibe to be in, with the intention to hold events here often. Being a boutique business our hours are Monday to Friday 9-5 however we are always happy to meet by appointment as needed.

    Thank you for all the support by my amazing clients, colleagues, friends and family over the years for helping me get to this point, and thank you for your interest in Marianetti Motors Inc.
